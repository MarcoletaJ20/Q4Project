The Philippines is referred to as the "Pearl of the Orient" in Asia due to the beauty of its natural landscape. Here are 3 places that you must visit in the Philippines

Bohol


Bohol is known for its famous Chocolate Hills and its tarsiers, which may be the world's smallest primate, it is also heritage sites and old stone churches.

Boracay


Boracay is a small island in the central Philippines, it's known for its resorts and beaches. It is also considered as one of the top destinations for relaxation.

Palawan


Palawan is a stunning province in the Philippines some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in the world. Contains crystal-clear waters to lush forests and stunning limestone cliffs.
