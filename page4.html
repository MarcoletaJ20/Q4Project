Ja Morant

The 2019-20 NBA Rookie of the Year started all 130 of his appearances in two seasons with the Memphis Grizzlies… Has averaged 18.4 points, 3.9 rebounds and 7.3 assists in 31.7 minutes while shooting 46.3 percent from the field, 31.7 percent from 3-point range and 75.0 percent from the free throw line… Has officially missed 14 games due to injury and one game for rest… Has scored in double figures 120 times, including 54 games with at least 20 points, nine games with at least 30 points and one game with at least 40 points… Has grabbed double-digit rebounds three times… Has recorded double-digit assists 31 times… Has posted 29 double-doubles, including three triple-doubles… His longest career streak of games scoring in double figures is 31 (Jan. 24-Dec. 26, 2020)… His longest career streak of games scoring at least 20 points is five (April 19-26, 2021)… His longest career streak of games with double-digit assists is three (twice, last from Feb. 10-14, 2021).
